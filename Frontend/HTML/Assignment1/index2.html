Editorial 1:

Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj (1630-1680) was the founder of the Maratha Empire, a legendary Indian warrior king known for his innovative guerrilla warfare, military genius, and just rule, establishing an independent kingdom against the powerful Deccan Sultanates and Mughal Empire, and pioneering maritime defense and a unique administration with an eight-minister council (Astapradhan). He was crowned Chhatrapati in 1674 at Raigad Fort, promoting Maratha pride, cultural identity, and religious tolerance, leaving a legacy as a visionary ruler inspiring India's navy and fostering regional self-rule.
